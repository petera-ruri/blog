BIO

About myself:

Kia ora, my name is Petera and I am 29 years of age.

I am of Maori/Cook island descent.

I was born in Rotorua, New Zealand but did most of my schooling on the Northsore of Auckland.

I have 2 kids,my eldest is Lennox and he is 4years old, my youngest name is Leeson and he is 3 years old.

I like to play sports such as rugby, touch, golf and table tennis.

I consider myself very determined, always willing to learn and adaptable to any situations aswell as a risk taker.
